Who We Are

We are an independent UK-based non-profit dedicated to using digital tools to help locate missing persons. Our team combines compassion and technology to reunite families and provide vital support in difficult times.

Our Mission

Technology & Innovation

Using OSINT, data analytics, and ethical digital profiling to uncover leads and assist searches.

Compassion & Support

We work hand-in-hand with families, providing clear communication and ongoing emotional support.

Every Life Matters

No case is too small. Every missing person is someone’s loved one—and deserves to be found.
