Who We Are

We’re a non-profit organisation committed to locating missing persons through digital investigation. With a team of skilled volunteers, we bring families hope and support using the power of modern technology.

Our Mission

Technology & Innovation

We use modern tools—OSINT, AI, social analysis—to uncover vital leads and help bring loved ones home.

Compassion & Support

We guide families with empathy and care, working closely with authorities to provide clarity and help.

Every Person Matters

Our mission is personal—every missing person is someone’s loved one. We never stop searching.
