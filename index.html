Who We Are

Digital Lifeline is an independent, non-profit organisation committed to locating missing persons in the digital era. Our dedicated team of trained volunteers and digital investigators collaborates closely with families, communities, and law enforcement across the UK to provide hope and support.

Our Mission

Technology & Innovation

We leverage advanced technology, social media investigation, and ethical online profiling to uncover vital information that may lead to sightings and reunions.

Compassion & Support

We partner with families, police, and organisations, offering empathy, clarity, and essential assistance during challenging times.

Every Person Matters

With every search, we aim to bring missing persons home, combining compassion with innovation to create meaningful impact.
